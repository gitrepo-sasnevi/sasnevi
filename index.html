About Sasnevi
Envision the Soil Health

Soil Health is defined as the suite of biological, chemical, and physical properties which enable soils to function as a vital living ecosystem that sustains all life above and underneath the soil surface.

Sasnevi envisions a future where farmers use soil health systems to reduce agriculture’s negative impacts on the environment, while simultaneously improving the long-term productivity of our soils and efficiency of local farming practices

Healthy soil is the basic source of healthy food production. Better Soil for Better Health.

Sasnevi Mission
Embrace, Explore, Conserve Soil
01
Embrace

Develop a farmer-to-farmer network to promote and educate farmers and the general public on the benefits of good soil health practices and its advantages

02
Explore

To promote and support the successful application of good soil health practices with necessary technology, to ensure that the tradition and culture of farming in India can be carried on to future generations

03
Conserve

To promote local farm trials and share the results to help farmers develop good soil health practices for their own farm operations for the sustainable environment
